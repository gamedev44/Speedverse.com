Welcome to Roadmap

A powerful, offline-first project planner.

  • Drag-like feel: Smooth horizontal scrolling.
  • Rich Media: Paste images directly from clipboard.
  • Markdown: Use **bold**, __underline__, and more.
  • Secure: Data stays in your browser.
Tip: On mobile, swipe left/right to navigate columns.

Help & Guide

Adding Columns: Scroll to the far right and click the big dashed box.

Adding Cards: Click "+ Add Card" at the bottom of any column.

Editing: Click the pencil icon or the card body.

Deleting: Open a card/column and click the red "Delete" button.
