Welcome to --Road-Map--

A powerful, offline-first project planner.

  • Responsive: Works on desktop and mobile.
  • Rich Media: Paste images directly from clipboard.
  • Markdown: # Headers, **Bold**, __Underline__.
  • Secure: Data stays in your browser.

Help & Guide

Adding Lanes/Columns: Scroll to the far right and click the big dashed box.

Adding Cards: Click "+ Add Card" at the bottom of any Lane.

Editing: Click the card body, or the pencil icon on the Lane header.

Deleting: Open a card/Lane and click the red "Delete" button.

### Normal Bold Text
